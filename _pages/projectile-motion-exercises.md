# 📝 Projectile Motion — Exercises
{: .section-heading }

Twenty problems to practice the equations from the [Projectile Motion Theory]({{ '/projectile-motion-theory/' | relative_url }}) page, arranged in five levels of increasing difficulty — from ⭐ (warm-up) to ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (expert). Unless stated otherwise, use \\(g = 9.81 \, \text{m/s}^2\\), neglect air resistance, and launch from ground level.
{: .theory-section }

> ✅ **Check your work**
> Every problem below is fully solved, step by step, on the [Solutions page]({{ '/projectile-motion-solutions/' | relative_url }}). Try to solve each exercise on your own first!
{: .math-jit-box }

---

## ⭐ Level 1 — Warm-up
{: .section-heading }

<div class="exercise-item tier-1" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**1.**</span> A ball is launched with an initial speed \\(v_0 = 20 \, \text{m/s}\\) at an angle \\(\theta = 30°\\) above the horizontal. Calculate the initial horizontal and vertical velocity components, \\(v_{0x}\\) and \\(v_{0y}\\).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-1" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**2.**</span> A projectile is launched at \\(\theta = 45°\\) with an initial velocity \\(v_0 = 30 \, \text{m/s}\\). Calculate its total time of flight \\(T\\).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-1" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**3.**</span> A stone is thrown with \\(v_0 = 15 \, \text{m/s}\\) at an angle \\(\theta = 60°\\). Find the maximum height \\(H\\) it reaches.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-1" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**4.**</span> A ball leaves a kicker's foot with \\(v_0 = 25 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 40°\\). Find the horizontal range \\(R\\).
</div>

---

## ⭐⭐ Level 2 — Getting Comfortable
{: .section-heading }

<div class="exercise-item tier-2" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**5.**</span> A javelin is thrown with \\(v_0 = 22 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 35°\\). Find its horizontal position \\(x\\) and height \\(y\\) at \\(t = 1.5 \, \text{s}\\).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-2" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**6.**</span> A soccer ball kicked at \\(\theta = 50°\\) lands \\(R = 18 \, \text{m}\\) away. Find the initial speed \\(v_0\\).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-2" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**7.**</span> A ball is thrown with \\(v_0 = 18 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 42°\\). Determine the magnitude and direction of its velocity vector at \\(t = 1.0 \, \text{s}\\).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-2" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**8.**</span> A basketball player shoots with \\(v_0 = 9 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 55°\\). Find the time needed to reach the maximum height and the maximum height itself.
</div>

---

## ⭐⭐⭐ Level 3 — Building Skills
{: .section-heading }

<div class="exercise-item tier-3" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**9.**</span> A projectile launched with \\(v_0 = 28 \, \text{m/s}\\) lands \\(R = 40 \, \text{m}\\) away. Find the **two** possible launch angles that produce this range.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-3" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**10.**</span> A ball is thrown **horizontally** (\\(\theta = 0°\\)) with \\(v_0 = 12 \, \text{m/s}\\) from the top of a \\(20 \, \text{m}\\) building. Find the time it takes to hit the ground and the horizontal distance it travels.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-3" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**11.**</span> A projectile launched at \\(\theta = 50°\\) lands back at the same height \\(T = 2.5 \, \text{s}\\) later. Find its initial speed \\(v_0\\), then compare the range it actually achieved to the maximum range it could achieve at the same speed launched at 45°.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-3" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**12.**</span> A rescue plane flying horizontally at \\(80 \, \text{m/s}\\) and an altitude of \\(150 \, \text{m}\\) drops a supply package. Find the horizontal distance the package travels before hitting the ground, and its impact speed.
</div>

---

## ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 4 — Challenge
{: .section-heading }

<div class="exercise-item tier-4" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**13.**</span> A golfer hits a ball from the edge of a \\(30 \, \text{m}\\) cliff with \\(v_0 = 25 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 20°\\) above the horizontal, out over the lower ground below. Find the total flight time and the horizontal range (measured from the base of the cliff).
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-4" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**14.**</span> A ball must be launched at \\(\theta = 55°\\) from ground level to just clear a \\(6 \, \text{m}\\) high wall located \\(15 \, \text{m}\\) away. Find the minimum initial speed \\(v_0\\) required.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-4" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**15.**</span> Two balls are launched from the same point with the same speed \\(v_0 = 25 \, \text{m/s}\\): ball A at \\(\theta = 30°\\), ball B at \\(\theta = 60°\\). Show that they land at the same spot, and find how much later ball B lands compared to ball A.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-4" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**16.**</span> A stone is released \\(1.5 \, \text{m}\\) above the ground with \\(v_0 = 14 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 25°\\), over level ground. Find the total flight time and the horizontal range.
</div>

---

## ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Level 5 — Expert
{: .section-heading }

<div class="exercise-item tier-5" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**17.**</span> A ball is launched from ground level with \\(v_0 = 45 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 38°\\) toward a fence \\(15 \, \text{m}\\) high located \\(x = 60 \, \text{m}\\) away. Compute the ball's height when it reaches \\(x = 60 \, \text{m}\\) and determine whether it clears the fence.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-5" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**18.**</span> A projectile is launched with \\(v_0 = 30 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 50°\\). Find the **two** times at which the projectile is at a height \\(y = 20 \, \text{m}\\), and explain what each solution physically represents.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-5" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**19.**</span> A cannon sits at the edge of a cliff \\(H = 60 \, \text{m}\\) above the sea and fires a projectile with \\(v_0 = 40 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 30°\\) above the horizontal, out over the water. Find the total flight time and the speed of the projectile when it hits the sea.
</div>

<div class="exercise-item tier-5" markdown="1">
<span class="ex-num">**20.**</span> A ball is launched from the base of a hill with \\(v_0 = 25 \, \text{m/s}\\) at \\(\theta = 50°\\). The hill rises from the launch point at a constant incline of \\(20°\\). Find the distance **along the incline** at which the ball lands.
</div>

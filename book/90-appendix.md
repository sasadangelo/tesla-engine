# Appendix A: Physical Constants and Units {.unnumbered}

The following constants are used throughout this book.

| Constant | Symbol | Value |
|---|---|---|
| Gravitational acceleration (Earth surface) | g | 9.81 m/s² |
| Speed of light in vacuum | c | 299 792 458 m/s |
| Pi | pi | 3.14159... |

## SI Units Used

| Quantity | Unit | Symbol |
|---|---|---|
| Length | metre | m |
| Time | second | s |
| Velocity | metres per second | m/s |
| Acceleration | metres per second squared | m/s² |
| Angle | degree / radian | ° / rad |

## Conversion Factors

- 1 km/h = 0.2778 m/s
- 1 mph  = 0.4470 m/s
- 1 degree = pi/180 rad approx 0.01745 rad
